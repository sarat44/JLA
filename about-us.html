About Us

What we are

JALA Technologies, a multifaceted company, carved a niche for themselves in IT training, staffing and software development in India. JALA Technologies was set up with a vision to fill the gaps between the academic education and the practical IT world in the current industry. We believe that every individual has their own strengths and weakness. We strive to bring out the best through skill enhancement techniques.
JALA Technologies area of focus and expertise is software training and its related services. Our training programs are designed to bring out the best of raw, young and talented resources through innovative, process-oriented and project-oriented training methodologies. We serve the corporate in providing the best and efficient solutions catering to their clients aboard.
The goal of our training program is to provide the world with skilled human resources who are perfectly competent to manage the client requirements through automation in an ever-changing technology world.

We train candidates who can perform par with that of a 3+ year experienced professional; that is the intensity of the course, which JALA Technologies delivers.
